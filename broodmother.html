Broodmother

Offlane, Mid

  • Spin Web

    Spins a large web that grants Broodmother a
    passive movement speed increase, and
    boosts regeneration while in its vicinity, as
    well as giving free movement.

  • Silken Bola

    Slows the target enemy, causes them to miss
    and deals impact damage. Units that attack
    the target deal bonus magic damage.

  • Spinner's Snare

    Broodmother may create up to 5 invisible
    web lines touching any existing web. Enemy
    Heroes that cross the web line will spring the
    trap, becoming rooted, revealed in fog, and
    take damage over time.

  • Spawn Spiderlings

    Broodmother injects her young into an
    enemy unit, dealing damage. The
    spiderlings will hatch if the target is killed
    while under this influence.
