    Drives Bloodseeker into a bloodthirsty rage

    Bloodseeker baptizes an area in sacred blood. After
    2.9 seconds the ritual completes, causing any
    enemies caught in the area to take damage and
    become silenced.

    Bloodseeker sprays his blood continuously in the
    area around him, damaging himself to damage and
    slow his enemies.

    Causes an enemy unit's skin to rupture, dealing
    initial damage based on its current health.
